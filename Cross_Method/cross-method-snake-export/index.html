How to play

Use arrow keys or WASD to guide the snake. Eat the correct factorization for the trinomial shown above the board. There is no time limit. You have 3 lives, clear 5 levels to win, and avoid walls or your own body.

Normal: eat the correct factor pair in one step. Advanced: first eat the common factor, then eat both correct linear factors.
